## About Terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox adventure game that blends exploration, crafting, building, and combat in a procedurally generated world. Players can dig, gather resources, construct structures, and fight a wide variety of enemies while progressing through different stages of gameplay.

The game offers freedom in how players approach their journey. Whether focusing on building creative structures, exploring underground caves, crafting powerful equipment, or battling bosses, Terraria provides a flexible experience shaped by player decisions.

With a large variety of items, enemies, environments, and progression systems, Terraria delivers a highly replayable sandbox experience centered around creativity, survival, and discovery.
